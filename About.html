About Us


Our aim is to controll the beach pollution

There are 5.25 trillion pieces of plastic waste estimated to be in our oceans. 269,000 tons float, 4 billion microfibers per km² dwell below the surface. 70% of our debris sinks into the ocean's ecosystem, 15% floats, and 15% lands on our beaches. In terms of plastic, 8.3 million tons are discarded in the sea yearly.So to overcome from this problem we basically discourage polluting the beach and incentivising awareness,we plan to implement a system using blockchain. For users who collect and bring trash to us will be incentivised with our token.For the violators caught polluting they will be fined heavily and a part of the fine will be given to the reporter who can report through our app and many more.
